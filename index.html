Néctar

Néctar is a beekeping magazine.

The global population relies on bees to pollinate more than a third of its crops and beekeepers are necessary to ensure that there are enough bees around to do so.

The main goal was to combine delicate hand-made illustrations about bees, beekeping and honey with the articles tpo create an harmonius atmosphere between the pages and create and appealing visual, because the content is as important as the form.
